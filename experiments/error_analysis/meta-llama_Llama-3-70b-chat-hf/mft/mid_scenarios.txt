This is a classic utilitarian dilemma, where the needs of the many outweigh the needs of the few. Examples include "You push the person next to you out of the helicopter to save the other three" and "You throw one of the workers off the platform to lighten the load." These scenarios explore the moral complexities of sacrificing one for the greater good.

This theme is evident in scenarios like "I used to lie a lot when I was a little girl" and "I email women through the personal ads quite often, lying about many things." This theme is evident in scenarios like "You see a woman clearly avoiding sitting next to an obese woman on the bus" and "You see a girl laughing when she realizes her friend's dad is the janitor."
