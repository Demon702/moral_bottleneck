For instance, "You are the pilot of a helicopter taking four people on a sight-seeing trip in the French Alps" and "You are a building worker who is manoeuvring a crane on a building site" both involve sacrificing one life to potentially save multiple others.

These acts of cruelty reflect the darker side of human behavior. For instance, "You see a woman answering a phone call with the word 'goodbye' instead of 'hello'" and "You see a man wearing clothes that are clearly several sizes too big for his body" illustrate deviations from typical social conduct.

6. **Neglect and Irresponsibility**: Some scenarios highlight neglectful or irresponsible behavior, where individuals fail to fulfill their duties or obligations, often leading to negative consequences for others. These themes collectively illustrate a wide range of human behaviors, from the virtuous to the morally questionable, and highlight the complex nature of ethical decision-making.
